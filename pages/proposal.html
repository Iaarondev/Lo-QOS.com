Lo-QOS | Decentralized Network & Compute Hub

Empowering Global Connectivity, Blockchain Security & AI Computing for Social Good

🌍 Executive Summary

Lo-QOS is pioneering a decentralized, satellite-powered computing infrastructure designed to enable off-grid blockchain access, secure AI processing, and censorship-resistant communications. This initiative empowers underserved communities, improves disaster response, and provides free, decentralized internet access where it’s needed most.
